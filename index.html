Diversity and Inclusion Principles

Eliminate discrimination, harassment and victimisation

A way for schools and businesses to eliminate discrimination is by making sure they comply with the Equality Act 2010.

Advance equality of opportunity

Having due regard to the need to remove or minimise disadvantages suffered by people due to their relevant protected characteristics

Foster good relations between different parts of the community

Fostering good relations between different parts of the community means taking action to reduce prejudice and increase understanding between groups of people

Best Practices

  • Use the “Inclusive Workplace" Model
  • Evaluate your executive team — do they portray diversity and inclusion?
  • Acknowledge and honor multiple religious and cultural practices
  • Foster a workspace culture where every voice is welcome, heard, and respected
  • Open a dialogue about gender pay inequality
